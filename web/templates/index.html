Understanding the Factors

Different HYROX venues have varying layouts, run loop lengths, and Roxzone distances. This tool uses statistical modeling to calculate course correction factors.

  • Normalized: Your time adjusted to the reference venue
  • Venue-to-Venue: Convert your time to what it would be at a different venue
  • Course Factor: Percentage showing how much harder/easier a venue is
